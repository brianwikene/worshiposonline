WorshipOS is a worship planning and team coordination platform built for churches. It's designed for the people who run Sunday morning — worship directors, kids coordinators, schedulers, and staff who care about getting the details right without losing sight of the people involved.

Most churches are up and running in less than an hour. You don't need to import your whole congregation to begin — many teams start with a few key leaders and grow from there.

## Is WorshipOS a good fit?

WorshipOS works well if your church:

- Schedules a worship team week to week
- Coordinates volunteers across multiple teams — kids, welcome, AV, security
- Wants to track attendance and connections without turning your congregation into a spreadsheet
- Is ready to retire the shared Google Doc for service planning

## Getting set up

### Step 1 — Create your church

You'll enter a few basics to get started:

- Church name
- Subdomain (e.g., `grace.worshipos.com`)
- Timezone

### Step 2 — Invite your first team members

Start with the people involved in planning gatherings — your worship leader, tech director, or service coordinator. You can add the rest of your team later.

### Step 3 — Create your first gathering

Add the next service or event on your calendar. It can be as simple as:

> Sunday Gathering — 10:00 AM

### Step 4 — Build your first Worship Flow

Add the elements that make up the service — songs, Scripture readings, announcements, message, prayer — and assign the people serving each part.

Once that's done, your whole team can collaborate around the same plan.
