WorshipOS is a worship planning and team coordination platform built for churches. It's designed for the people who run Sunday morning — worship directors, kids coordinators, schedulers, and the staff who care about getting the details right without losing sight of the people involved.

## Who it's for

WorshipOS is a good fit if your church:

- Has a worship team you schedule week to week
- Uses volunteers across multiple serving teams (kids, welcome, AV, security)
- Wants to track attendance and connections without turning your congregation into a spreadsheet
- Is tired of managing service order in a shared Google Doc

## Setting up your church

After signing up, you'll be guided through:

1. **Adding your church details** — name, subdomain, and timezone
2. **Inviting your first team members** — start with your worship team or staff
3. **Creating your first gathering** — a Sunday service, a special event, whatever's next on the calendar
4. **Building your first service flow** — songs, Scripture readings, announcements, and the people serving each piece

You don't need to import your full congregation to get started. WorshipOS works well with even a handful of people — you can grow the database as you go.
